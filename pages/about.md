## About the Collection

This is a collection of family photos, curated to focus on the women in my family during childhood and moments of personal success. I was interested in what the photos reveal about the changing cultural perceptions of girlhood. What was worth photographing? What was appropriate to photograph?

Last year, my mom inhereted a box of photos when her dad passed away. She spent a month digitizing all of the photos, and then was too exhausted to do anything else. I wanted to help her organize them, so that she could use them in her crafts and other projects. This collection is also my final project for Information Organization.
